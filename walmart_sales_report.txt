Walmart Sales Analysis Report

Executive summary
The dataset contains 6,435 store-week records across 45 stores and $6.74B in total sales. Sales are concentrated in a smaller set of stores, and the strongest predictors of weekly sales are Store, CPI, unemployment, temperature, and fuel price.

Key findings
- Total sales: $6.74B
- Average weekly sales: $1.05M
- Holiday weeks: about 7.0 percent of records
- Regression model R2: 0.960
- Classification model accuracy: 0.947
