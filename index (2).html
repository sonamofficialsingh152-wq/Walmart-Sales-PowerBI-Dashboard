What the analysis shows

Sales concentration

A small group of stores contributes a disproportionate share of revenue, so store-level ranking is essential for operational focus.

Time patterns

Weekly trend and holiday context are the best views for explaining movement in sales across the calendar.

Business drivers

Store identity, CPI, unemployment, temperature, and fuel price are the strongest predictors of weekly sales.

Modeling summary

Regression

Random forest regression reached R² = 0.960, which is a strong fit for weekly sales forecasting.

Classification

High-vs-low sales classification reached 94.7 percent accuracy with excellent class separation.

Interpretation

The model is useful for prioritization and forecasting, with store-level and macro-economic variables carrying most of the signal.
